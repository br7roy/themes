From the Artist

November 11th, 2003

Danger Mouse 2004 by Gedeon Maheux

It all comes full circle. My original set of Danger Mouse icons were released way back in 1997 and were some of the very first classic icons I created. The set is based on a popular British cartoon for kids of the same name. It followed Danger Mouse and his trusty side-kick Penfold on adventures akin to James Bond, only their missions were much funnier and somehow always seemed to go wrong. Danger Mouse usually fought his arch-nemesis Baron Silas Greenback and his pet Nero, although there were occasional guest villans that would make appearances from time to time.
